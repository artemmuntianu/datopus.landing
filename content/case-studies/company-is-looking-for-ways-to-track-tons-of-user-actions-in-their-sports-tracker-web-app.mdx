### The Challenge

TrackFit, a growing company developing a **sports tracker web app**, needs a powerful analytics solution to monitor and optimize user engagement, workout tracking, and performance insights. Their web app serves **<code>1,000</code> daily active users** and **<code>30,000</code> monthly active users**, each generating **<code>1,000</code> events per day**, totaling **<code>30 million</code> events** per month. 

To improve user experience and app performance, TrackFit wants to track everything that can be useful in such an application, including:
- **User Engagement:** Session durations, screen visits, feature usage.
- **Workout Tracking:** Logs of workouts, types of exercises, intensity levels.
- **Performance Metrics:** Calories burned, distance covered, heart rate data (if integrated with wearables).
- **Social Features:** Friend activity, leaderboards, challenge participation.
- **Subscription & Monetization:** Subscription sign-ups, plan changes, churn analysis.
- **Device & Connectivity:** Mobile vs. desktop usage, offline tracking.

TrackFit's team seeks a **cost-effective** solution that **minimizes setup complexity and maintenance**, ensuring their developers focus on product improvements rather than manual event tracking.

### The Evaluation Process

TrackFit evaluates six product analytics platforms: **Datopus, Statsig, Posthog, Woopra, Mixpanel, and Amplitude.** Their key criteria include:

- **Cost efficiency** – Can the platform support <code>30M</code> events at a reasonable price?
- **Comprehensive tracking capabilities** – Can it track workouts, performance data, social engagement, and monetization metrics?
- **Ease of integration and maintenance** – How much effort is required for setup and ongoing management?
- **Scalability** – Can the platform handle future growth without exponential cost increases?
- **Data source compatibility** – Does it integrate seamlessly with existing tools like Google Analytics?
- **Support & flexibility** – Does the platform offer good support and customization options?

### Pricing Comparison

For **<code>30,000</code> users and <code>30M</code> events per month**, the pricing structure for each platform is:

- **Datopus** – $50
- **Statsig** – $1,400
- **Posthog** – $2,059
- **Woopra** – $5,999
- **Mixpanel** – $8,120
- **Amplitude** – $252

At **$50 per month**, Datopus remains the most cost-effective option, while Amplitude offers a competitive alternative at **$252 per month**. However, competitors like Mixpanel and Woopra cost significantly more, reaching over **$8,000 per month**.

### Why TrackFit Chose Datopus

After evaluating the available options, TrackFit concluded that **Datopus** was the best solution due to the following key advantages:

1. **Unbeatable Pricing:** At **$50 per month**, Datopus is the most cost-effective option, saving TrackFit **thousands of dollars** compared to competitors.
2. **Tracks Everything That Matters:** Unlike some competitors, Datopus enables tracking of **workouts, performance data, user engagement, social interactions, and monetization metrics**.
3. **Minimal Setup & Maintenance Effort:** With **event auto-tracking**, Datopus reduces engineering workload compared to platforms that require extensive manual setup.
4. **Scalability Without Cost Explosions:** Datopus provides **predictable, budget-friendly pricing**, unlike competitors that charge exponentially more as event volume grows.
5. **Seamless Integration with Google Analytics:** TrackFit leverages Datopus's **Google Analytics and BigQuery support** to unify their data streams effortlessly.
6. **24/7 Customer Support & Customization:** Unlike some competitors, Datopus provides **round-the-clock support and customizable analytics solutions** tailored to TrackFit's needs.

### The Outcome

By choosing **Datopus**, TrackFit now has **a complete view of user activity and workout tracking insights while saving thousands of dollars annually**. The team can now **optimize user engagement, improve retention, and refine monetization strategies** without dealing with high costs or complex setups.

For any company developing a **sports tracker web app**, **Datopus is the clear winner** for its **affordability, ease of integration, and comprehensive tracking capabilities**.
